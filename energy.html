New Energy

When talking about "new energy" I mean the kind of energy that can allow all humanity, not only the western countries, to be able to reach self-sustainability while producing 100% clean energy. What does it mean? It means that the new energy source must:
  • use no combustion: we don't need to produce pollution, we don't even need to just reduce it, we need to stop it, to put an end to it
  • use no water (like nuclear power plants): we already lack of drinkable water for everyone, we don't need to waste it
  • use no radioactive material: no fission → no artificially built harmful radiations → no problem
  • use no solar panels: I know, solar panels seem so great, but how are they produced? And what happens when you have to dispose of them? (read about it)
  • work both on Earth and in space: we are about to become a multi-planetary specie, so we need generators to work well and safely in space and work just fine even without solar power
  • be manufacturable without polluting and be 100% recyclable

I started this project identifying a big problem I wanted to solve: SMOG. Once realized than the whole thing is the result of the lack of innovation int he energy sector I started to think about new ways to produce energy. After a while I decided the way to go and created many designs. First in my mind, then as simple scratches and finally as 3D models, always improving the final outcome in the chances of success and the amount of material needed to build it (I couldn't spend much). When I reached design number 3 (called "Mark 3") I approached the prototyping phase and failed. The design didn't work... at all. Fortunately, I learned very much from that attempt. I went back in hardware mode and remodeled the design, twice. The Mark 5 will see the light soon enough.

Further details will be disclosed as soon as possible.
